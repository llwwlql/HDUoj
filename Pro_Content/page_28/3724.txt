<div class="plm"><table align="center"><tr><td><b>Time Limit:</b> 10000MS</td><td width="10px"></td><td><b>Memory Limit:</b> 65536K</td></tr><tr><td><b>Total Submissions:</b> 3270</td><td width="10px"></td><td><b>Accepted:</b> 682</td></table></div><p class="pst">Description</p><div class="ptx" lang="en-US"><div><p>Panda is preparing for the mid-term examination by reading his notebook. However, some parts of his notebook have been ruined and some data are lost. For example, when he encounter a list of functions, he find out that all parameters are lost. The functions are similiar and can be written as </p><p><i>y</i> = exp(<i>a<sub>0</sub></i><b>x</b>) + exp(<i>a<sub>1</sub></i><b>x</b>) + ... + exp(<i>a<sub>9</sub></i><b>x</b>).</p><p>Although all the <i>a<sub>i</sub></i> are lost, the coordinates of some points on the curve of the function are known. Can you find back all the <i>a<sub>i</sub></i> with these coordinates? Notice that all the <i>a<sub>i</sub></i> are positive integers no more than 10 and <i>a<sub>i</sub></i> &le; <i>a<sub>i+1</sub></i> (i=0,1,...,8). It is guaranteed to been only one correct answer. </p></div></div><p class="pst">Input</p><div class="ptx" lang="en-US"><div><p>The first line of input is <i>N</i>, the number of the coordinates.<br>Each of the next <i>N</i> lines contains a pair of coordinates <i>x<sub>i</sub></i>, <i>y<sub>i</sub></i> separated by one space. </p><p>1 &le; <i>N</i> &le; 20<br>0 &le; <i>x<sub>i</sub></i> &le; 5 <br></p></div></div><p class="pst">Output</p><div class="ptx" lang="en-US"><div><p>The output contains 10 lines, each contains one <i>a<sub>i</sub></i> in ascending order. </p></div></div><p class="pst">Sample Input</p><pre class="sio">20
