He has picked up <i>N</i> girls and <i>M</i> boys and wants to collect them to be his soldiers. To collect a soldier without any privilege, he must pay 10000 RMB. There are some relationships between girls and boys and Windy can use these relationships to reduce his cost. If girl <b>x</b> and boy <b>y</b> have a relationship <i>d</i> and one of them has been collected, Windy can collect the other one with 10000-<i>d</i> RMB. Now given all the relationships between girls and boys, your assignment is to find the least amount of money Windy has to pay.
