<div class="plm"><table align="center"><tr><td><b>Time Limit:</b> 4000MS</td><td width="10px"></td><td><b>Memory Limit:</b> 65536K</td></tr><tr><td><b>Total Submissions:</b> 405</td><td width="10px"></td><td><b>Accepted:</b> 18</td></table></div><p class="pst">Description</p><div class="ptx" lang="en-US"><div><p>As the standoff with Empire's Army continues, the Commander of Union, Yang, finally loses his patience. He intends to send a troop going through the long straight blockade line secretly and spying into enemy's activities. </p><p>After several days' observation, the Commander finds the following facts: <ul><li>The blockade line is <i>L</i> meters long and there are <i>N</i> Empire's soldiers patrolling along the line.</li><li>The soldiers move one meter per second.</li><li>Each soldier's vision covers a range of <i>R</i> meters, both in front of and behind him.</li><li>When a soldier meets (the soldier meets, not his vision meets) an end of the line, he turns around and continues his patrol.</li></ul></p><p>The whole break-through will last <i>T</i> seconds. To make sure the action successes, Yang wants to maximize the average uncovered length during the <i>T</i> seconds. In another word, assuming the function <i>f</i>(<i>t</i>) indicating the uncovered length of the blockade line at time <i>t</i>. Yang wants to maximize the definite integral:</p><p><img src="formula?tex=I%28x%29%3D%5Cint_%7Bx%7D%5E%7Bx%2BT%7D+f%28t%29dt+%5Cqquad+%28x+%5Cgeq+0%29%E3%80%80" alt="I(x)=\int_{x}^{x+T} f(t)dt \qquad (x \geq 0)　" align="absmiddle"></p></div></div><p class="pst">Input</p><div class="ptx" lang="en-US"><div><p>The first line of input contains four integers, <i>L</i>, <i>R</i>, <i>N</i>, <i>T</i> (0 &le; <i>L</i>, <i>R</i>, <i>T</i> &le; 1000000), (0 &le; <i>N</i> &le; 100).</p><p>The following <i>N</i> lines describe the soldiers. Each line consists of two integers, <i>x</i> (0 ≤ <i>x</i> ≤ <i>L</i>) and <i>d</i>. <i>x</i> indicates the initial position of the soldier and <i>d</i> indicates the direction of soldier's movement. The soldier moves to the end with<i> x = L</i> when <i>d</i> = 1 and to the end with <i>x</i> = 0 when <i>d</i> = -1.</p></div></div><p class="pst">Output</p><div class="ptx" lang="en-US"><p>Output the maximum <i>I</i>(<i>x</i>) with three digits after the decimal point.</p></div><p class="pst">Sample Input</p><pre class="sio">10 1 1 3
1 1
</pre><p class="pst">Sample Output</p><pre class="sio">25.000</pre><p class="pst">Source</p><div class="ptx" lang="en-US"><a href="searchproblem?field=source&key=POJ+Monthly+Contest+%E2%80%93+2009.02.22">POJ Monthly Contest – 2009.02.22</a>, Dagger</div></td></tr></table>
