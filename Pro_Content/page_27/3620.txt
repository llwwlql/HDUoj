His insurance agency will only repay him, however, an amount depending on the size of the largest &quot;lake&quot; on his farm.<br><br>The farm is represented as a rectangular grid with <i>N</i> (1 &le; <i>N</i> &le; 100) rows and <i>M</i> (1 &le; <i>M</i> &le; 100) columns. As one would expect, a lake has a central cell to which other cells connect by sharing a long edge (not a corner).
