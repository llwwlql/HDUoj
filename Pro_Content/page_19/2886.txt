<div style='position: absolute; right: 10px;'>Language:<select size=1 onChange="window.location.href='problem?id=2886&lang='+this.value+'&change=true'"><option value='default' selected>Default</option></select></div><div class="ptt" lang="en-US">Who Gets the Most Candies?</div>
<div class="plm"><table align="center"><tr><td><b>Time Limit:</b> 5000MS</td><td width="10px"></td><td><b>Memory Limit:</b> 131072K</td></tr><tr><td><b>Total Submissions:</b> 13742</td><td width="10px"></td><td><b>Accepted:</b> 4351</td></tr><tr><td colspan="3" align="center" style="color:#0ead00"><b>Case Time Limit:</b> 2000MS</td></tr></table></div><p class="pst">Description</p><div class="ptx" lang="en-US"><p><i>N</i> children are sitting in a circle to play a game.</p><p>The children are numbered from 1 to <i>N</i> in clockwise order. Each of them has a card with a non-zero integer on it in his/her hand. The game starts from the <i>K</i>-th child, who tells all the others the integer on his card and jumps out of the circle. The integer on his card tells the next child to jump out. Let <i>A</i> denote the integer. If <i>A</i> is positive, the next child will be the <i>A</i>-th child to the left. If <i>A</i> is negative, the next child will be the (<span lang="en-us">&minus;</span><i>A</i>)-th child to the right.</p><p>The game lasts until all children have jumped out of the circle. During the game, the <i>p</i>-th child jumping out will get <i>F</i>(<i>p</i>) candies where <i>F</i>(<i>p</i>) is the number of positive integers that perfectly divide <i>p</i>. Who gets the most candies?</p></div><p class="pst">Input</p><div class="ptx" lang="en-US">There are several test cases in the input. Each test case starts with two integers <i>N</i> (0 &lt; <i>N</i> <span lang="en-us">&le; 500,000) and <i>K</i> (1 &le; <i>K</i> &le; <i>N</i>) on the first line. The next <i>N</i> lines contains the names of the children (consisting of at most 10 letters) and the integers (non-zero with magnitudes within 10<sup>8</sup>) on their cards in increasing order of the children&rsquo;</span>s numbers, a name and an integer separated by a single space in a line with no leading or trailing spaces.</div><p class="pst">Output</p><div class="ptx" lang="en-US"><p>Output one line for each test case containing the name of the luckiest child and the number of candies he/she gets. If ties occur, always choose the child who jumps out of the circle first.</p></div><p class="pst">Sample Input</p><pre class="sio">4 2
Tom 2
Jack 4
Mary -1
Sam 1</pre><p class="pst">Sample Output</p><pre class="sio">Sam 3</pre><p class="pst">Source</p><div class="ptx" lang="en-US"><a href="searchproblem?field=source&key=POJ+Monthly--2006.07.30">POJ Monthly--2006.07.30</a>, Sempr</div></td></tr></table>
