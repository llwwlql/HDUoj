<div class="plm"><table align="center"><tr><td><b>Time Limit:</b> 1000MS</td><td width="10px"></td><td><b>Memory Limit:</b> 65536K</td></tr><tr><td><b>Total Submissions:</b> 2043</td><td width="10px"></td><td><b>Accepted:</b> 840</td></table></div><p class="pst">Description</p><div class="ptx" lang="en-US">A long, linear field has N (1 <= N <= 1,000) clumps of grass at unique integer locations on what will be treated as a number line.Think of the clumps as points on the number line.<br><br>Bessie starts at some specified integer location L on the number line (1 <= L <= 1,000,000) and traverses the number line in the two possible directions (sometimes reversing her direction) in order to reach and eat all the clumps.  She moves at a constant speed (one unit of distance in one unit of time), and eats a clump instantly when she encounters it.<br><br>Clumps that aren't eaten for a while get stale.  We say the "staleness" of a clump is the amount of time that elapses from when Bessie starts moving until she eats a clump.  Bessie wants to minimize the total staleness of all the clumps she eats.<br><br>Find the minimum total staleness that Bessie can achieve while eating all the clumps.</div><p class="pst">Input</p><div class="ptx" lang="en-US">* Line 1 : Two space-separated integers: N and L.<br>* Lines 2..N+1: Each line contains a single integer giving the position P of a clump (1 <= P <= 1,000,000).<br></div><p class="pst">Output</p><div class="ptx" lang="en-US">* Line 1: A single integer: the minimum total staleness Bessie can achieve while eating all the clumps.</div><p class="pst">Sample Input</p><pre class="sio">4 10
1
9
11
19
</pre><p class="pst">Sample Output</p><pre class="sio">44
</pre><p class="pst">Hint</p><div class="ptx" lang="en-US">INPUT DETAILS:<br>Four clumps: at 1, 9, 11, and 19. Bessie starts at location 10.<br><br>OUTPUT DETAILS:<br>Bessie can follow this route:<br>* start at position 10 at time 0<br>* move to position 9, arriving at time 1<br>* move to position 11, arriving at time 3<br>* move to position 19, arriving at time 11<br>* move to position 1, arriving at time 29</div><p class="pst">Source</p><div class="ptx" lang="en-US"><a href="searchproblem?field=source&key=USACO+2005+November+Gold">USACO 2005 November Gold</a></div></td></tr></table>
