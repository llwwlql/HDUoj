<div class="plm"><table align="center"><tr><td><b>Time Limit:</b> 1000MS</td><td width="10px"></td><td colspan="3"><b>Memory Limit:</b> 65536K</td></tr><tr><td><b>Total Submissions:</b> 5537</td><td width="10px"></td><td><b>Accepted:</b> 2182</td><td width="10px"></td><td style="font-weight:bold; color:red;">Special Judge</td></tr></table></div><p class="pst">Description</p><div class="ptx" lang="en-US"><p><i>Beer Lovers Club</i> makes regular parties. They hate warm beer, but club’s refrigerator is too small to store enough beer for the whole company. So they decided to order a special super-big beer refrigerator. The new refrigerator should be a parallelepiped <i>a</i> × <i>b</i> × <i>c</i> and store exactly <i>n</i> cubical 1 × 1 × 1 beer boxes (the club has <i>n</i> members). To decrease losses of cold, the total area of the surface of the refrigerator must be as small as possible.</p><p>For example, if the capacity of the refrigerator must be 12, the possible variants are:</p><table align="center" border="0"><tr><td align="center">Dimensions</td><td style="width: 1em;"></td><td align="center">Surface Area</td></tr><tr><td align="center">3 × 2 × 2</td><td style="width: 1em;"></td><td align="center">32</td></tr><tr><td align="center">4 × 3 × 1</td><td style="width: 1em"></td><td align="center">38</td></tr><tr><td align="center">6 × 2 × 1</td><td style="width: 1em;"></td><td align="center">40</td></tr><tr><td align="center">12 × 1 × 1</td><td style="width: 1em;"></td><td align="center">50</td></tr></table><p>The best variant in this case is 3 × 2 × 2.</p><p>Help the beer lovers to find the optimal dimensions of their new refrigerator.</p></div><p class="pst">Input</p><div class="ptx" lang="en-US"><p>The input file contains single integer number <i>n</i> (1 &le; <i>n</i> &le; 10<sup>6</sup>) — the capacity of the refrigerator. Help the beer lovers to find the optimal dimensions of their new refrigerator.</p></div><p class="pst">Output</p><div class="ptx" lang="en-US"><p>Output three integer numbers: <i>a</i>, <i>b</i> and <i>c</i> — the optimal dimensions of the refrigerator.
