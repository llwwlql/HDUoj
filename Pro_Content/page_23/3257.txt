<div class="plm"><table align="center"><tr><td><b>Time Limit:</b> 2000MS</td><td width="10px"></td><td><b>Memory Limit:</b> 65536K</td></tr><tr><td><b>Total Submissions:</b> 2842</td><td width="10px"></td><td><b>Accepted:</b> 1123</td></table></div><p class="pst">Description</p><div class="ptx" lang="en-US"><p>The cows are building a roller coaster! They want your help to design as fun a roller coaster as possible, while keeping to the budget.</p><p>The roller coaster will be built on a long linear stretch of land of length <i>L</i> (1 &le; <i>L</i> &le; 1,000). The roller coaster comprises a collection of some of the <i>N</i> (1 &le; <i>N</i> &le; 10,000) different interchangable components. Each component <i>i</i> has a fixed length <i>W<sub>i</sub></i> (1 &le; <i>W<sub>i</sub></i> &le; <i>L</i>). Due to varying terrain, each component <i>i</i> can be only built starting at location <i>X<sub>i</sub></i> (0 &le; <i>X<sub>i</sub></i> &le; <i>L </i>-<i> W<sub>i</sub></i>). The cows want to string together various roller coaster components starting at 0 and ending at <i>L</i> so that the end of each component (except the last) is the start of the next component.</p><p>Each component <i>i</i> has a &quot;fun rating&quot; <i>F<sub>i</sub></i> (1 &le; <i>F<sub>i</sub></i> &le; 1,000,000) and a cost <i>C<sub>i</sub></i> (1 &le; <i>C<sub>i</sub></i> &le; 1000). The total fun of the roller coster is the sum of the fun from each component used; the total cost is likewise the sum of the costs of each component used. The cows' total budget is <i>B</i> (1 &le; <i>B</i> &le; 1000). Help the cows determine the most fun roller coaster that they can build with their budget.</p></div><p class="pst">Input</p><div class="ptx" lang="en-US">Line 1: Three space-separated integers: <i>L</i>, <i>N</i> and <i>B</i>.<br>Lines 2..<i>N</i>+1: Line <i>i</i>+1 contains four space-separated integers, respectively: <i>X<sub>i</sub></i>, <i>W<sub>i</sub></i>, <i>F<sub>i</sub></i>, and <i>C<sub>i</sub></i>.</div><p class="pst">Output</p><div class="ptx" lang="en-US">Line 1: A single integer that is the maximum fun value that a roller-coaster can have while staying within the budget and meeting all the other constraints. If it is not possible to build a roller-coaster within budget, output -1.</div><p class="pst">Sample Input</p><pre class="sio">5 6 10
0 2 20 6
2 3 5 6
0 1 2 1
1 1 1 3
1 2 5 4
3 2 10 2</pre><p class="pst">Sample Output</p><pre class="sio">17</pre><p class="pst">Hint</p><div class="ptx" lang="en-US">Taking the 3rd, 5th and 6th components gives a connected roller-coaster with fun value 17 and cost 7. Taking the first two components would give a more fun roller-coaster (25) but would be over budget.</div><p class="pst">Source</p><div class="ptx" lang="en-US"><a href="searchproblem?field=source&key=USACO+2006+December+Silver">USACO 2006 December Silver</a></div></td></tr></table>
