<div class="plm"><table align="center"><tr><td><b>Time Limit:</b> 1000MS</td><td width="10px"></td><td colspan="3"><b>Memory Limit:</b> 65536K</td></tr><tr><td><b>Total Submissions:</b> 5507</td><td width="10px"></td><td><b>Accepted:</b> 1401</td><td width="10px"></td><td style="font-weight:bold; color:red;">Special Judge</td></tr></table></div><p class="pst">Description</p><div class="ptx" lang="en-US">In the newest census of Jersey Cows and Holstein Cows, Wisconsin cows have earned three stalls in the Barn of Representatives. The Jersey Cows currently control the state's redistricting committee. They want to partition the state into three equally sized voting districts such that the Jersey Cows are guaranteed to win elections in at least two of the districts.<br><br>Wisconsin has 3*K (1 <= K <= 60) cities of 1,000 cows, numbered 1..3*K, each with a known number (range: 0..1,000) of Jersey Cows. Find a way to partition the state into three districts, each with K cities, such that the Jersey Cows have the majority percentage in at least two of districts.<br><br>All supplied input datasets are solvable.</div><p class="pst">Input</p><div class="ptx" lang="en-US">* Line 1: A single integer, K<br><br>* Lines 2..3*K+1: One integer per line, the number of cows in each city that are Jersey Cows.  Line i+1 contains city i's cow census.</div><p class="pst">Output</p><div class="ptx" lang="en-US">* Lines 1..K: K lines that are the city numbers in district one, one per line<br><br>* Lines K+1..2K: K lines that are the city numbers in district two, one per line<br><br>* Lines 2K+1..3K: K lines that are the city numbers in district three, one per line</div><p class="pst">Sample Input</p><pre class="sio">2
510
500
500
670
400
310</pre><p class="pst">Sample Output</p><pre class="sio">1
2
3
6
5
4</pre><p class="pst">Hint</p><div class="ptx" lang="en-US">Other solutions might be possible.  Note that "2 3" would NOT be a district won by the Jerseys, as they would be exactly half of the cows.</div><p class="pst">Source</p><div class="ptx" lang="en-US"><a href="searchproblem?field=source&key=USACO+2005+February+Gold">USACO 2005 February Gold</a></div></td></tr></table>
