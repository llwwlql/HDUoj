<div class="plm"><table align="center"><tr><td><b>Time Limit:</b> 1000MS</td><td width="10px"></td><td><b>Memory Limit:</b> 65536K</td></tr><tr><td><b>Total Submissions:</b> 315</td><td width="10px"></td><td><b>Accepted:</b> 85</td></table></div><p class="pst">Description</p><div class="ptx" lang="en-US">To save money you are considering renting a small cube van to transport your belongings to the Big City. The interior of the van is a rectangular box with width w, height h, and length l. The box has a sliding door that lifts but only to height H. That is, there is an immovable overhang of height H-h at the top of the door. <br>You have a large rectangular box that you wish to load on the truck. Can you get it on the truck subject to the following constraints: <br><ul><li>The box must fit through the door, tilting it forward or sideways (but not both) as necessary (see figure below). <br><li>The box must be placed with one side flat against the floor. <br><li>The box must be placed with one side flat against the front wall. <br><li>The door must close once the box is in place. </ul><br>You may assume there are no obstructions (such as a ceiling or the ground) outside the truck that might interfere with loading. </div><p class="pst">Input</p><div class="ptx" lang="en-US"><img src=images/2468_1.jpg align=right>There are several test cases, each represented by two lines of input. The first line of each contains 4 integers: w, h, l, H. The next line contains 3 integers - the dimensions of the box.</div><p class="pst">Output</p><div class="ptx" lang="en-US">For each test case, print "The box goes on the truck." if it is possible to load the box on the truck; otherwise print "The box will not go on the truck." You may assume that you start with an empty truck for each test case. </div><p class="pst">Sample Input</p><pre class="sio">8 8 12 7
8 12 8
8 8 12 7
7 12 8
8 8 12 7
1 7 13
100 200 99 190
100 195 30
</pre><p class="pst">Sample Output</p><pre class="sio">The box will not go on the truck.
The box goes on the truck.
The box will not go on the truck.
The box goes on the truck.
</pre><p class="pst">Source</p><div class="ptx" lang="en-US"><a href="searchproblem?field=source&key=Waterloo+local+2001.09.29">Waterloo local 2001.09.29</a></div></td></tr></table>
